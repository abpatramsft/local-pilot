# System Design Best Practices

1. Define clear requirements and objectives before starting design.
2. Choose scalable architectures to handle growth and future needs.
3. Prioritize modularity and separation of concerns for maintainability.
4. Use appropriate data storage and caching strategies.
5. Design for fault tolerance and graceful error handling.
6. Ensure security and privacy are integrated from the start.
7. Document design decisions and rationale for future reference.
8. Plan for monitoring, logging, and observability.
9. Optimize for performance, but avoid premature optimization.
10. Review and iterate design based on feedback and testing.
